•Developed a novel approach for the synthesis phenolic lipids. This versatile methodology grants complete control over the position of unsaturation in the lipid tail and its geometric isoform.

•Used a redox-neutral dual photocatalytic system for the generation of adamantyl radicals which were used in Giese-type additions to imines to create products of medicinal relevance.
